Overview:

During this study you will be faced with a set of mazes of varying difficulty and paired with a robot of varying capability. At any time you may choose how the robot is controlled: you can drive the robot (Manual control), or let the robot automatically drive (Automatic control). You will have to watch out for driving over dangers which could make you and the robot fail the maze. Your view of the mazes, and control you have of the robot will be limited, so you must work together with your new robot partner to complete each maze.

You will be presented with a display to help navigate the maze:



At any time during the maze, take control the robot yourself by clicking Manual Control. Use the Up, Left, Right, and Down buttons to drive:

At any time during the maze, let the robot drive itself to the finish (to the best of its ability) by selecting Automatic Control. You can end Automatic Control at any time by pressing the Manual Control button:



There will be glass like debris in the maze. If the robot is driving in Automatic Control, and it drives over this debris, it could move randomly in any direction:



But watch out! If the robot falls in a hole you will fail the maze:



If you don't think you can complete the maze you can abort it:



Scoring

You will receive a score based on how well you and the robot perform on each maze. At the end of the HIT you will be paid a bonus based on your final score and the effort you put into the experiment. Each maze is worth 5 bonus points. Your goal should be to maximize points based on the below scoring breakdown:
  • Fail maze (robot falls in a hole): -5 points
  • Abort maze (press abort button): -3 points
  • Drive in Manual Control: -0.1 points per manual step

Summary:

  • The robot has more information about the maze than you do
  • Manual Control allows you to drive with the up/down/left/right buttons
  • Automatic Control lets the robot drive to the finish (to the best of it's abilities)
  • Glass may cause the robot to move in an unexpected direction
  • Falling in a hole fails the maze
  • You can abort if you are unable to get the robot to the finish
