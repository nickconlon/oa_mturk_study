You have been paired with a new robot.

This robot is capable of sending you a report telling you its self-confidence in driving to an intermediate location denoted by a blue square, and its self-confidence in driving from that intermediate location to the finish. This statement is what the robot thinks its outcome will be if it drives in Automatic Mode. It can be: 'very bad', 'bad', 'fair', 'good', or 'very good'. For example:

  • A report of very bad means the robot is certain it will fail the maze.

  • A report of fair means the robot thinks there is an equal chance for failure or success.

  • A report of very good means the robot is quite certain it will succeed in driving to the finish.

Remember, this is the robot's estimate of how it thinks it will perform in the maze in Automatic Mode, not how it thinks you will perform in Manual Mode. The report will be displayed just above the maze:
